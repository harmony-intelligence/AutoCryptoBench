How it works

Tier Points

Earn points by submitting transactions.

Transactions Minimum Points
0 - 3 $6.00 100
4 - 10 $6.00 90
11 - 50 $6.00 75
51 - 100 $6.00 50
101 - 500 $6.00 25

Volume Bonus

Earn additional points when you submit a transaction of a certain size.

Volume Points
$1,000.00 - $10,000.00 10
$10,000.00 - $100,000.00 20
$100,000.00 + 45
Check-Ins

Earn daily and weekly bonus points on transactions eligible for Tier Points.

Check-In Points
Daily 5
Weekly 45
Referrals

Earn 10% of your referral's Tier Points for every transaction they submit.

Quests

Earn points when you complete the requirements for a Quest Task. For example, a Bridge Task requires a transaction across two different chains.

*Minimum of 300 points is required to claim rewards
